## What buyers expect at ~£333/month

**Must-haves (universal across UK comparators):**
- Tailored training plan
- Nutrition coaching / macro guidance
- Progress tracking
- Regular check-ins
- App delivery (NKPT, TJ, UP, AS Personal Training, Matt Justice — all use apps)

**Tier-separating cadence:**
- Most: weekly/twice-weekly **async** check-ins (text/app/messenger)
- **Live weekly calls = premium feature, NOT default** even at 1:1
- NKPT: 2× weekly + IM
- TJ: weekly in-app + messenger
- Sarah Louise low: text every 2 weeks; high: weekly video calls
- Matt Justice: weekly progress + monthly video form review

**App vs WhatsApp split (market norm):**
- App for **structure + data**
- Messaging for **accountability**
- Calls for **milestone reviews**
- ⚠️ NOT "everything lives in WhatsApp" — that pattern is gone

**Community:** add-on, not the lead value driver for £997 1:1 (it IS lead value for cohort like 12WBT).

## Recommended £997 / 12-week offer structure (deep-research's spec)

| Component | Recommended |
|---|---|
| **Positioning** | One clear niche + outcome (e.g. fat loss for busy professionals, postpartum strength, **South Asian body transformation**) |
| **Onboarding** | 45-60 min strategy call + questionnaire + baseline photos/measurements + written 12-week roadmap |
| **Training** | Fully personalised, in-app, exercise videos + progression logic |
| **Nutrition** | Personalised cals/macros + meal framework + food-environment guidance + check-in adjustments. **NOT rigid 7-day meal plan** unless audience asks |
| **Check-ins** | Weekly async with structured review of adherence, body data, obstacles |
| **Live support** | **3-4 live calls across 12 weeks**: onboarding, early adjustment, mid-point, week-12 close/renewal |
| **Messaging** | In-app primary, weekday response promise, **WhatsApp ONLY for urgent** |
| **Accountability** | Habit tracking, steps, sleep, training compliance, form-check video review |
| **Community** | Optional — group chat / challenge board / broadcast — add-on, not core |
| **Renewal** | Step-down to **£299-£349/mo continuity** OR second 12-week block with loyalty discount |

> Critical operational note: "If Mukund wants to keep the programme profitable at 15-25 active clients, the offer should feel high-touch but be built on app-first systems and milestone calls, NOT an 'always-on' concierge service."

## Funnel benchmarks (replaces brief assumptions)

### Application form completion — HIGH CONFIDENCE
- **Zuko benchmarks** (across all forms): 45% complete, 55% abandon
- Application-form-specific: **51.6% overall, desktop 53.9%, mobile 45%**
- **Plan range: mid-40s to mid-50s.** Below = friction, weak motivation, too many fields, poor mobile UX.

### Discovery call close rates — MEDIUM CONFIDENCE
- Joanna Lott Coaching: **25-40%** (use 25% conservative)
- Expert CFO: 10-30%
- Tirion service-pro funnels: 25-40% close, **75-80% show on static pages**, **88-92% show on conversational funnels**
- ⇒ **25-40% strong but realistic. 10-20% = warning zone.**

### Profile → Lead — LOW CONFIDENCE
- Tirion: 1-3% follower-to-bio-click, 35-55% page engagement, 40-60% qualification, 65-80% booking of qualified leads, 25-40% close

### Recommended forecasting ranges

| Stage | Low | Mid | High |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Profile / bio visitor → lead | 10% | 25% | 45% |
| Lead → completed application | 20% | 35% | 50% |
| Application started → submitted | 40% | 50% | 60% |
| Submitted application → attended call | 30% | 50% | 70% |
| Attended discovery call → close | 15% | 25% | 35% |

## Retention / renewal — LOWER CONFIDENCE

- My PT Hub: **65-70% annual PT retention** (general industry)
- Ardent GSI: **40% client loss by month 3** without retention system
- TJ: clients with larger goals stay 9-12 months
- **Planning rule**:
  - 30-50% renewal into continuity = reasonable
  - <25% = problem in expectation-setting / programme / renewal design
  - \>50% = very strong for a fixed-term offer

Hyperbound: **structured sales coaching can lift close rate by 35%** — strong case for a disciplined consultation script, not a "friendly chat".

## Risks acknowledged in this analysis

1. **Transparency bias** — premium coaches don't publish prices
2. **Model mismatch** — "online coaching" spans cohorts, marketplaces, app subs, hybrid, bespoke 1:1
3. **Funnel denominator inconsistency** — different sources count different steps
4. **Retention data scarcity** — public 12-week renewal benchmarks are weak
