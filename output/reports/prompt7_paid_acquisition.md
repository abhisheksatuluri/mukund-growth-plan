# Prompt 7 Output — Paid Acquisition Benchmarks for UK Online Fitness Coaching

> Source: ChatGPT Deep Research, delivered 2026-04-26 by user. Triangulated from broad paid-media benchmarks, health-and-wellness landing page benchmarks, application-form completion data, official Meta/Google guidance, and UK local-search estimates.

## Executive view

For a UK online fitness coach with **30k IG followers + growing YouTube** in 2025-2026:

> The most realistic first move is **NOT broad cold acquisition**. It's a **warm-audience stack**: Instagram engagement → DM qualification → application → instant booking, supported by Meta + YouTube retargeting, with Google Search reserved for bottom-funnel local-intent demand capture.

Economics are favourable **only if the funnel is already healthy**. Wellness landing pages typically convert at **5.1% median** (8.2% in wellness subcategory). Application forms complete at **51.6% overall** with a noticeable mobile penalty. Where coaches lose money: AFTER the application, when manual follow-up replaces an instant calendar step.

**Shorthand:** Meta/IG warm = cheapest controllable lead source. YouTube retargeting = solid mid-funnel layer (NOT first standalone scale channel). Google Search = bottom-funnel capture, hardest on cash flow.

> **The cheapest lever is rarely the ad platform — it's the application-to-booked-call rate.** Coaches who feel "ads don't work" usually have a thank-you page problem, not a Meta problem.

## Recommended test budget + sequencing

**For a coach making £5-10k/mo:**

| Phase | Budget | Channel | Trigger |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Phase 1 | £900-£1,500 / 30 days | Meta warm audiences + DM funnel | Default starting point |
| Phase 2 | £200-£500 / month | YouTube retargeting + website retargeting | Once retargeting lists active (100+ users) |
| Phase 3 | £1,000-£2,000 separate test | Google Search "PT London" type | ONLY after application + booking flow proven |

**Total Phase 1 buys:** ~35-70 Meta/DM applications, 30-60 YouTube-retargeting applications, OR 18-45 Google Search applications.

- **Below £1k total** → test ends before enough data
- **Above £3k unproven** → over-spending relative to early-stage funnel

## Go / no-go criteria for paid traffic

**Ready to scale paid:**
- ✅ 5%+ visit-to-application rate on warm traffic
- ✅ 50%+ application completion rate
- ✅ 40%+ application-to-booked-call (with INSTANT scheduler)
- ✅ 20-25%+ close rate on booked calls

**Don't run paid yet if:**
- ❌ Offer is still soft / positioning vague
- ❌ Organic content doesn't already generate meaningful DMs
- ❌ Cannot follow up immediately
- ❌ Mobile application UX is rough
- ❌ Cannot afford to lose first £1.5-£2.5k while learning
- ❌ Splitting small budget across Meta + Search + YouTube simultaneously (creates illusion all are mediocre)
