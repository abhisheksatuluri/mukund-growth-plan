# Strategy Summary — Mukund Venkat → £10k/month

> Snapshot date: 26 April 2026 · Corpus: last 50 IG posts (30 Jan → 25 Mar 2026) · Followers: 29,206

## TL;DR

The £10k goal is a **conversion + positioning** problem, not an audience-size problem. Mukund already has 29k followers and ~£5k/month from PT — the gap is closeable in 90 days by tightening the funnel and launching one corporate pilot.

**Three findings that change the plan:**

1. **Conversion leakage is the #1 issue.** Only **1 in 50 posts** has an "apply / DM ELITE" CTA. He has the audience; he isn't asking for the sale.
2. **HYROX is his strongest organic pillar.** All three HYROX-themed carousels are in the top 6 by engagement. The "Indian + Ghanaian lions in London" framing is differentiated and not generic fitness content.
3. **Mindset content is overweight and underperforms.** 27 of 50 posts (54%) are mindset/motivation but most sit in the bottom half of views (lowest reels = 81–172 views). It is filler, not lead-gen.

## Recommended path: Scenario B (£10,485/month, recognised)

| Stream | Action | Monthly contribution |
|---|---|---|
| In-Person PT | Lift effective rate via packages + selective +£10/session | £5,500 |
| Online Coaching | Build to 15 active clients (~5 new/month, 3-month tenure) | £4,985 |
| Corporate | Optional upside; 1 pilot in M2-M3 | +£1,000–£2,000 |

This decouples revenue from new-sales velocity, gives a churn buffer, and is reachable from current organic reach if conversion mechanics improve. Scenario A (cash, upfront) is the alternative if cash matters more than predictability.

## Top 3 risks

1. **Audience composition unknown.** Bio + content suggest UK/global; comments include heavy non-UK names. If <30% of audience is UK, an in-person/corporate London pivot needs paid retargeting.
2. **Compliance drift.** Several posts use phrases like "Sick people are profitable" and reference fasting/nutrition without disclaimers. ASA/CAP exposure if scaled.
3. **Single-channel dependency.** No newsletter, no lead magnet, no captured email list. If IG reach drops, the funnel collapses.

## Top 3 immediate actions (this week)

1. **Add a CTA to every reel.** Pin the "DM ELITE" line as the last 2 seconds + first comment + caption close. This alone could 5-10x DM volume.
2. **Build a one-page application landing page + form.** Replace the generic Linktree with a single-purpose form. Use the 11-question script from §21 of the brief.
3. **Pick ONE niche pillar for 30 days.** Recommend: "Hybrid fitness for busy London professionals" — combines his proven HYROX angle with a clearer ICP.
