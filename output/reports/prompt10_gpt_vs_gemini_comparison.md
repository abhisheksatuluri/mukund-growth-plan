## Synthesis: don't pick — A/B test 3 candidates, not 2

The original Gemini protocol tested **D vs A**. Given GPT's strong case for C (executive longevity), the better test is **3-way: D vs C vs A** over 60 days with appropriate budget split.

### Why 3-way A/B is justified
1. Gemini's confidence on D depends on Mr. India authority assets being prominently surfaced — a real-world test will reveal whether London consumers respond
2. GPT's confidence on C depends on the website framing converting cold traffic — testable
3. A is the safe baseline against which both extremes can be measured
4. The cost increment of running 3 ad sets vs 2 is marginal (~50% more budget); the strategic value of avoiding the wrong permanent rebrand is high

### Recommended budget split
- £750/month each on D + C + A landing pages and ad creative sets (≈£2,250/month total — sits within the £1,500-£2,500 Phase 1 paid budget per Prompt 7 Phase 1)
- Identical pricing on all three landing pages (£997 / 12 weeks)
- Distinct calendar booking links for attribution
- 60 days of cold paid + 14 days organic Trojan Horse content rotation
