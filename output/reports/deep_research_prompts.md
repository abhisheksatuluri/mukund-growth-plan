# Deep Research Prompts — Mukund Venkat £10k/month Roadmap

These are copy-paste-ready prompts for a deep research agent (Claude Research, Perplexity Pro, ChatGPT Deep Research, etc.). They are ordered by leverage on the £10k goal.

Each prompt expects: **structured output, source URLs, publication dates, evidence quality rating, and an explicit "verified vs. inferred vs. assumption" classification**.
