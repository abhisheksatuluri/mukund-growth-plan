## ⭐ 60-Day A/B Validation Protocol

Gemini's recommended test architecture before committing to permanent rebrand:

### Phase 1 — Days 1-14 — Infrastructure
- Build 2 separate landing pages (URL A = Body Transformation; URL B = South Asian Executive)
- Both at identical £997 / 12-week pricing (controlled variable)
- Build 2 Meta ad creative sets:
  - **Set A (Transformation)**: time-efficiency, executive stress, dramatic aesthetic results, luxury London b-roll
  - **Set B (South Asian Niche)**: "Why traditional Western diets fail Indian genetics" / familial insulin resistance / cultural-career balance / Mr. India authority markers
- Distinct calendar booking links for attribution

### Phase 2 — Days 15-30 — Cold paid traffic
- Identical daily Meta budget, isolated ad sets
- Ad Set A: affluent London professionals (financial pubs, premium gyms, luxury apparel interests)
- Ad Set B: same affluence/geo + South Asian culture, international tech, expatriate affinity markers
- KPIs daily: CPC, CTR, CPL, **application quality scoring** (income, occupation, willingness to invest £997)
- Hypothesis: D will achieve drastically lower CPC + higher CTR due to hyper-specificity disrupting fitness-ad blindness

### Phase 3 — Days 31-45 — "Trojan Horse" organic rotation
Test warm IG audience appetite WITHOUT abruptly changing bio.
- **Week 5**: deploy reels on South Asian metabolic advantage/disadvantage, cultural diet hacks, diaspora high-achiever psychology. Track save + share rates (algorithmic resonance).
- **Week 6**: contrasting reels on executive body transformation, time management, physical accountability.
- IG Stories interactive polls: "What is your biggest hurdle right now? A: No time to train due to executive workload. B: Struggling to out-train a traditional cultural diet."

### Phase 4 — Days 46-60 — Qualitative synthesis + decision
- Track sales-call objections per funnel
- Calculate ROAS + CAC for both
- **Decision matrix**: if D yields lower CAC + higher show rate + less £997 price resistance → permanent pivot. Update bio, website, all brand architecture immediately.
