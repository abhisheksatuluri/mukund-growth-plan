## Sponsorship & affiliate thresholds

**Tier framework (Shopify 2026 + CreatorsJet 12,486 campaign dataset):**
- Nano: 1K-10K followers
- Micro: 10K-100K followers
- Pricing increasingly depends on engagement, saves, shares, usage rights, format

**Three practical thresholds for Mukund:**

| Stage | Realistic when |
|---|---|
| Affiliate + gifting | Can start surprisingly early if niche fit good. Nano creators already have IG/TikTok/YouTube rate ranges in CreatorsJet data |
| **First paid sponsorships** | Micro-tier presence — IG/TikTok 10K-50K followers + solid engagement; YouTube 10K-50K subs + reliable long-form. **Mukund's 29K IG = qualified by reach but ER (0.44%) is the gap.** |
| Recurring sponsor income | Channel needs stable audience + predictable performance. Brands now buy outcomes/quality, not just size. |

**Priority order for Mukund:** coaching sales first → affiliate second → sponsorship third → native payouts fourth.

## Best lead magnets for fitness coaching

The recurring patterns in current public UK fitness funnels:

1. **Scorecards / calculators** — macro, TDEE, strength standards. Immediate result + naturally sets up "here's what to do next" conversation.
2. **Checklists** — show-day, weekly, habit. Concrete and actionable for defined audience.
3. **Audits** — free 3-day nutrition audit, training/form audit. Strong for warm traffic (diagnoses problem vs gives tips).
4. **Mini-programmes / starter guides** — free weight-loss guide, recipe pack, quick-start framework, short challenge.

## Two-step CTA structure (vs one-step "apply now")

> **problem diagnosis → quick win → application / call**

Cold traffic doesn't convert off "Apply for £997 coaching" — it responds to a diagnostic or guided next step.

**Best CTA options:**
- "Get your personalised fitness audit"
- "Take the scorecard and see what's holding your progress back"
- "DM 'COACHING' and I'll send the 3-point action plan"
- "Book a 15-minute strategy call to map your next 12 weeks"
- "Download the starter guide, then apply if you want help implementing it"

**Channel-specific CTA fit:**

| Platform | CTA pattern |
|---|---|
| YouTube | Application/call CTAs + guide/audit CTAs in descriptions/pinned comments (viewer is warm) |
| Instagram | DM keyword CTAs + Story replies (natural progression in messaging layer) |
| TikTok | Comment keyword CTAs or profile-link to free guide (NOT hard close — audience colder) |
| LinkedIn | Professional framing — "Book a performance audit", "Download the desk-worker energy scorecard" |

## ⭐ Verified weekly production blueprint

**Mon-Tue**: Record ONE pillar YouTube video, 8-20 mins, on a monetisable theme:
- Fat loss for busy professionals
- Why your calorie deficit stalled
- Weekend overeating
- Strength for desk workers
- What actually changes in 12 weeks of online coaching

This is the trust asset AND the clip bank.

**Wednesday**: Publish YouTube + cut into:
- 2-4 Shorts
- 2 Instagram Reels
- 1 TikTok-native version
- 1 LinkedIn post (rewritten for professionals — NOT chopped from video)

LinkedIn version translates insight into work-performance frame: energy, consistency, burnout, leadership discipline, travel-friendly training, executive health.

**Thu-Fri** — Conversation layer:
- IG Stories with polls / question boxes / DM keyword CTA
- LinkedIn comment engagement + warm follow-ups
- Reply-to-comment videos on TikTok
- YouTube community post or pinned comment linking to lead magnet/application

**Weekend** — Push one deeper conversion asset: guide / calculator / audit / application CTA.

> Goal: not maximum posting volume, but enough repeated expert exposure to create **one or two meaningful sales conversations a week**.

## Revenue-first metrics dashboard (replaces vanity metrics)

### YouTube
- Views from search + suggested
- Avg view duration + retention on long-form
- Clicks to lead magnet/application from descriptions + pinned comments
- Consultation bookings attributed to YouTube

### Instagram
- Reel shares + saves (NOT likes)
- Profile visits per Reel
- Story replies
- DMs started from content
- Booked calls from DM conversations

### TikTok
- Profile visits per post
- Comment rate
- Link clicks / migration to IG/YouTube
- Qualified leads (NOT views)

### LinkedIn
- Profile views from content
- Newsletter / lead magnet sign-ups
- Inbound messages from professionals
- Corporate enquiries
- Booked meetings

### Overall commercial
- Lead magnet opt-in rate
- Consultation show-up rate
- Call-to-client close rate
- New client revenue by content source
- Revenue per 1,000 views by platform
- Payback time from each content pillar

> **North-star metric:** qualified sales conversations generated per week. Followed by closed revenue attributed to content.
