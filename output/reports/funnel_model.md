## 2. In-Person PT Funnel — Target: +£500–£1,500/month without more hours

Three levers, ranked by reversibility:

| Lever | Monthly delta | Risk | Reversibility |
|---|---:|---|---|
| Hold hours, +£10/session for new clients only | +£433 (in M1) → +£1,082 (steady state) | Low | Easy |
| 6 clients on £1,500/12-week packages instead of pay-per-session | +£750 | Med | Hard once signed |
| 4 × 2:1 sessions/week at £40pp (= £80/hr vs £55/hr) | +£693 | Low | Easy |
| Cap PT at 22 hrs, redirect 5 hrs to online sales | -£700 PT, +£X online | Med | Hard |

**Recommended**: combine first three. Avoid blanket price hike on existing clients; grandfather them at £55, charge £75 on new starts.

## 3. Corporate Funnel — Target: 1 pilot by M3, 1 retainer by M4

| Stage | Volume/month |
|---|---:|
| Targeted prospects (LinkedIn + email) | 150 |
| Meaningful engagements (likes, comments, replies) | 45 |
| Replies | 15 |
| Discovery calls | 8 |
| Proposals | 3 |
| Pilots closed | 1 |
| Retainers (by M3-M4) | 1 |

### Outreach cadence

- **Mon-Fri**: 30 LinkedIn touchpoints/day (target: HR Directors, People Ops Leads, Founders of 50-200 person London firms)
- **Tue + Thu**: 1 LinkedIn post on workplace wellbeing
- **Wed**: bulk-email 25 prospects with a specific case-study angle

### Corporate offer ladder

1. **Lunch-and-Learn** (£500-£1,500) — 60-min seminar on energy, stress, hybrid fitness. Foot in door.
2. **4-Week Energy Reset Challenge** (£1,500-£3,000) — team challenge with weekly check-ins. Pilot product.
3. **Monthly Wellbeing Retainer** (£2,000-£5,000) — 1 workshop + 1:1 coaching for execs + Slack support. Recurring revenue.
4. **Executive Cohort** (£3,000-£10,000) — 12-week transformation for 5-10 senior leaders.

Land with #1 or #2; expand to #3.

## 4. Content Funnel — Target: 1,575 profile visits/month from content

| Asset | Frequency | Job | Metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| YouTube long-form | 1 / week | Trust, depth, conversion | Avg watch time, link clicks |
| Shorts/Reels (cut from long-form) | 5-8 / week | Discovery | 3-second hold, profile clicks |
| Instagram Reels (original) | 3 / week | Pillar reinforcement | Profile visits |
| TikTok | 3 / week | Reach (lower intent) | New follower cost |
| LinkedIn | 2 / week | Corporate positioning | DM replies from HR titles |
| Email/Newsletter | 1 / week | Owned channel, churn-proof | Open rate, click-to-application |

### Required CTA discipline (current weak spot)

Every post must end with one of:

- **DM "ELITE"** (online coaching)
- **DM "TEAM"** (corporate)
- **Apply via link in bio** (high-intent posts)
- **Comment "AUDIT"** for the free scorecard (lead magnet)

If a post can't carry a CTA without feeling forced, it's filler — cut it. Mukund's bottom 5 reels (all generic mindset, 81-172 views) are exactly this.
